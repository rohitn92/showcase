Kaleidoreads

Design Brief: This project was a way to study the history of different Typefaces, Type Foundaries and Designers. The information is to be presented through the Web Application, Kaleidoreads, an immersive reading experience where we combine Classic English Literature with Typography of its time (or same era).

Typeface Selection

The following list of fonts were selected for the project, which will keep on increasing as more books are added.

  • Garamond 1500
  • Caslon 1700
  • Baskerville 1757
  • Bodini 1700
  • Gill Sans 1928
  • Franklin Sans 1902
  • Franklin Gothic 1902
  • Akzidenz Grotesque 1896
  • Avener 1988
  • Din 1931
  • Meta 1991
  • Georgia 1993
  • Gotham 2000
  • Bembo 1929
  • Didot 1780s
  • Claredon 1845
  • Halvetica 1957

Wireframes

The wireframes were built for mobile, tablet and desktop - keeping in mind how the interactions and experience will change with different form factors. Reading experience needs to be confortable with changing sizes, so a lot of attention was paid to font-sizing and text arrangements.

Visual Design

The visual design color uses a subtle hue of yellow-organe gradient. The primary fonts are Baskerville and Caselon. Each title is assigned its own cover image to suit the book.
